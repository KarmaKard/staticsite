Kustomer Karma

Best Deals. Anytime. Anywhere.

Ever had a deal card you had to carry with you to even use? Who has space for more plastic in their wallet or purse? Even if you do lug it around, you’re limited to use it a few times in a handful of businesses in certain zipcodes.


KarmaKard allows you to have deals where ever you are in the form of a mobile app. Our coupons app isn’t limited by the size of a card, so we will continually be adding more and more deals. You can share deals with friends, see how much you save, sort and filter deals to get the discounts you want! You will have access to deals wherever our karma network is built. Out of city or out of state? No problem.

Business Karma

Connect with Customers...and Their Data

Ever offered great deals to fundraising organizations only to be left empty handed with nothing in return?


With KarmaKard, each time a customer redeems a deal, you hear about it. We give you the ability to collect targeted customer data for strategic business marketing. You will be able to stay connected to loyal customers by sending them “Kwik Karma” or limited offers to those who like your business with the ability to collect data on the response. By giving great deals you get increased exposure, customer base, and powerful information to help drive business decisions.

Fundraiser Karma

Safe, Easy, and Trackable Fundraising

Lets face it, door to door fundraising is unsafe, ineffective, difficult to track and a waste of time.


KarmaKard allows cash or credit payments and tracks who sells what to whom, where, when and how. Track each individual fundraisers progress and the amount of cash they have raised. We allow you to extend past the front door to online. KarmaKard helps you email, share, like, tweet or post to any social media platform. Now Aunt Sally can donate all the way from Wisconsin. KarmaKard streamlines the whole process so that as soon as you create an account, you can see the karma coming in the form of Kash!
